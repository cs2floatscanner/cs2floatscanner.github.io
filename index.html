Features

Everything you need to trade smarter

One extension. Instant data. No sign-ups, no queues, no external servers.

🔍

Float & Paint Seed

See Float Value and Paint Seed right next to every market listing. Visual wear bar with precise marker position. Color-coded by wear range (FN/MW/FT/WW/BS). Copy any value with one click.

🎭

Stickers & Keychains

Automatic sticker detection with name, image and wear percentage. Keychain pattern display. Combo badge detection for 4x/5x matching pristine stickers.

💎

Blue Gem & Rare Patterns

Built-in database of 40+ rare patterns: Case Hardened Blue Gems for AK-47, Karambit, M9 Bayonet and 15+ knives. Heat Treated tiers. Color scales for 20+ charms.

📈

Technical Analysis

Full-screen interactive price chart with SMA, EMA, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, OBV. Drawing tools for lines. Custom time periods. Outlier filtering.

🔮

Price Forecast

Holt-Winters smoothing model predicts item price one month ahead. Three scenarios: optimistic, base and pessimistic. See where the price is heading before you buy or sell.

💲

Sticker Prices & Overpay

See the current market price for every applied sticker. Total sticker value and overpay estimate calculated automatically. Helps identify underpriced items with expensive stickers.

📉

Deep Order Recommendations

Analyzes 30 days of sales to suggest optimal buy order prices. Two strategies: aggressive (max profit) and moderate (more fills). Trend correction for rising markets. Target quantity support.

🔔

Float Alerts

Set a float threshold and get instant notifications when a matching item appears. In-page toast, audio beep, browser notification. Auto-scroll and highlight on the page.

Real Screenshot Card

Export a high-quality item card with a real in-game screenshot via Swap.gg integration. Share on social media or Discord with one click.
